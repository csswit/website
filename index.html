About us:

Have you ever had a programming project you wanted to attempt, but never knew where to start? Come join the CSS club for our weekly meeting. Here we discuss projects we want to try, demo projects we have already completed, have programming workshops and even guest speakers! Wentworth Computer Science Society (CSS@WIT) was founded as a branch of Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). Whether you're a master hacker or a script kitty, all skill levels are welcome in the hope of enhancing our skills to make ourselves more effective programmers. We also organize and execute wentworth's offical hackathon, Hack WIT us.
